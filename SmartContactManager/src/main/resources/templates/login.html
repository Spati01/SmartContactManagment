Contact Management

Contact management is the process of collecting, storing, organizing, and managing contact data. This data typically includes information about customers, leads, and other business connections. Efficient contact management enables personalized communication, better customer service, and improved sales opportunities.

Scalability issues

Even the best contact management software can fail to accommodate the needs of large organizations that have multiple teams spread across different locations. They don't have the basic functionalities needed for proper collaboration, data scrutiny, or even data protection for businesses that operate on a large scale. MNCs tend to have complex procedures and compliance requirements that need workflows in place, which contact management systems can't achieve alone.
